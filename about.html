Our History

From Humble Beginnings to Ethiopia's Best

Eagles Football Club Academy was established in 2015 by former yohannes legesse. What began as a small program in gelan with just 15 players has grown into one of Ethiopia's football academies.

Our founder recognized that Ethiopian football was rich in raw talent but lacked structured development pathways. They built Eagles FC Academy to bridge that gap — creating a professional environment where young Ethiopians can dream big, train hard, and achieve their full potential.

Today, Eagles FC Academy operates training programs across all age groups (under 15), has produced 50+ players, won our first trophie

Mission & Vision

What Drives Us

🎯

Our Mission

To discover and develop exceptional football talent from Ethiopia's youth, providing world-class coaching and opportunities that transform lives through the beautiful game. We are committed to producing technically excellent, mentally resilient, and culturally proud Ethiopian footballers.

🔭

Our Vision

To become one of football academy in ethiopia, recognized globally for producing quality players who represent Ethiopia with excellence, and to contribute to the transformation of Ethiopian football at all levels — from grassroots to the advanced.

🏆

Our Goals

To produce excellence players . To establish academies in Ethiopian cities. To partner with clubs for player exchange programs. To build Ethiopia's first dedicated youth football performance center in ethiopia.

Ethiopian Football Development

Proud of Our Ethiopian Roots

Ethiopia has a proud athletic tradition, producing some of the world's greatest distance runners. We believe the same dedication, resilience, and national spirit that defines Ethiopian athletics can fuel a football revolution. Our players train with the pride of the Ethiopian flag in their hearts.

We work closely with the regional sports bureaus, and local schools to identify talent in every corner of adiss and beyond. Every Ethiopian child deserves the opportunity to play, learn, and grow through football.
